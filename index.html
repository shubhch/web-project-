Our History

In its ICT-enabled Innovation and Entrepreneurship Program, Bot2Do focuses primarily on business incubation as a tool for creation of a competitive SME sector. This business incubator will provide us the fledgling businesses with shared access to infrastructure such as information and communication technologies, and access to business services, finance, mentoring and coaching. We enable job and small enterprise creation, as well as the commercialization of innovation with immense social value, such B2C products as Selection of Spectacles and Face recognition based attendance system. Bot2Do is now facing increased demand for leveraging its experience to catalyze the startup and strengthening of business to create specialized knowledge on particular types of businesses – notably those that are focused on Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, SaaS and Artificial Intelligence, as well as those that focus on high growth ICT enterprise development; and to continue to enable intra-organizational knowledge sharing and partnership opportunities between its members.
